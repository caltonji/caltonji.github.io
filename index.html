Portfolio

Recent Projects

NocNoc

With a team of friends I'm working on an android application that runs in the background of a device and notifies the user of their most recent notifications when knocked twice.
The application uses the sensoriomoter and audio capabilities of the phone to provide a similar experience that a pebble watch or other wearable computing devices do at virtually no cost.

Samplify

At MHacks III, with two friends, I created an Android application called Samplify. The app could listen to a song that was playing and recognize it by it's audio footprint and then tell you all of the songs that it sampled from. We wrote the app in 36 hours and released it on the Sunday after the hackathon ended

What started out as a Hackathon project turned into a whirlwind of an experience when our app started getting popular. Our website had over 100,000 unique hits and we recieved over 8,000 downloads in the 6 days that our app was on the app store. We were rated one of the top 5 hackathon projects at MHacks, the biggest university hackathon in the country.

Automate Innovation Vehicle Design

Senior year of High School I was team leader for an engineering competition in which we modified a children's car to drive itself through a maze and navigate obstacles by using GPS, motion, and rotation sensors on the wheels to decide it's path. We were awarded first place in the Square One Autonomous Vehicle Design competition.

Bluetooth Android Application
